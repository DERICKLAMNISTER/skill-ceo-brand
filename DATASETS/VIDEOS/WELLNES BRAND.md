This direction is full of energy, attitude, and more youth oriented. The names include blaze, instant power and intensity. Good spark, a natural spark of energy. Twist, inspired by the twist to release mechanism. The client chose mood board 1 and the name blaze. By week 5 we were showing three logo design concepts. Logo 1, a bold B mark blending honey heritage with clean energy. Premium minimal performance lead. Logo 2, the B, remagin' this lightning with strong color blocking and condensed type. Fast, modern, powerful. Logo 3, a B formed from the letter B, simple and onable paired with high inspired shapes to express natural energy in a modern way. Out of these three options the client liked the gradient visual identity of option 1 and the font used in option 2 but still felt we haven't quite nailed the symbol yet. By week 7 we're into design refinement. Logo 1, a performance lead direction where the letter B mirrors the dispensing cap, emphasizing function and portability. Logo 2, a geometric hive system that delivers natural energy cues through a bold repeatable pattern. Logo 3, the B, becomes a flame. Blending honey, heat and energy into a powerful instant impact logo. Option 3 was the final design the client went for.

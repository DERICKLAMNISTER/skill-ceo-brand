How to brand an online startup from scratch. Meet BlockCeller, a name with a vision. They aim to create an online platform for investment grade wine, with bottles worth up to $500,000. They needed a brand identity to match the sophistication of their audience. The product, BlockCeller, is building a secure blockchain-enable platform to buy, sell, and trade investment grade wine. The audience, we targeted premium wine collectors, who were also tech savvy and who appreciated the art and history behind each bottle. Their story, wines are stored in barrels at their respective wineries and only bottled upon purchase, ensuring integrity, authenticity, and provenance. Thanks to technology, they uncoork the future. The personality, BlockCeller, is the visionary, cutting edge, knowledgeable, and approachable. Now, based on this framework, we created three mood boards, elegant, minimalistic, modern, luxurious, rich, and warm. Clean, sophisticated, and refined. The client chose the first mood board. Now we designed three logo options. First logo type was inspired by blockchain connectivity, with nodes and networks. The second logo type had bracket-like designs for precision and protection. The third logo type had rich patterns inspired by wine racks. This was the logo that the client liked. We showcased the brand across website, mobile, and social media. BlockCeller is now poised to revolutionize the investment grade wine market. Follow for more premium branding advice.

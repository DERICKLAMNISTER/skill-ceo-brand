How to choose the perfect color palette for your logo? Utilize color harmonies. These are formulas for crafting visually appealing color combinations. Here's how they work. Analogous harmony. Choose one key color and then select colors on either side of it. Complementary harmony. Choose two colors that are directly opposite from each other on the color wheel. Split complementary harmony. Choose three colors that are evenly spaced on the color wheel. Tetradic harmony. Choose four colors that form two complementary pairs.
